Key Goals & System Objectives

Functional Requirements

CORE FEATURES

Publish & Manage Inventory

Enable airlines to define flight schedules, routes, and manage seat inventory dynamically across different aircraft types.

Search Flights

High-performance search capability filtering by source, destination, date, and fare classes with low-latency response times.

Seat Selection & Booking

Interactive seat maps allowing users to select specific seats, view pricing per seat type, and lock selections temporarily.

Ticket Generation

Automated generation of e-tickets with PNR upon successful payment, delivered via email and SMS services.

Cancellations & Refunds

Self-service cancellation workflows with automated refund calculation based on fare rules and policy engines.
