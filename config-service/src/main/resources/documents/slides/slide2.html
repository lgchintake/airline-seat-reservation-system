Problem Statement

XYZ Airlines System Design

Core Requirements

  • Global Operations: Support for both domestic and international flight
  • Inventory Management: Different fare classes (Economy, Business, First) with real-time seat inventory.
  • Booking Lifecycle: End-to-end flow from search and seat selection to booking, payment, and ticket issuance.
  • Post-Booking: Robust handling of cancellations, refunds, and rescheduling scenarios.

Business Context

XYZ Airlines aims to modernize its legacy infrastructure to handle high concurrency during peak travel seasons. The new system must ensure zero double-bookings while maintaining low-latency search results for millions of users.

Critical Integrations

Payment Gateways
PCI-DSS Security
Realtime Seat Locking
Notification Service
